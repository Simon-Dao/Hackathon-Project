Virtual Inventories

Businesses may often be in a surplus of supply. Even in the Covid pandemic, supply chain obstructions have rendered the management of inventory more difficult and convoluted. Demand often fluctuates arbitrarily at times, and business owners may not know how to manage their inventory accordingly.

Zaiko allows restaurants to have a virtual inventory, with many bonus features auspicious to management success.

Elimate Overproduction

Eliminate overproduction with Zaikos. Zaikos keeps track of all the items you have in your inventory and performs calculations to optimize your inventory quantity. It also notifies you when an item’s shelf life in your inventory will expire soon. With a few simple clicks, your inventory will be translated to a virtual interface.

By having Zaikos as your digital inventory, you can eliminate overproduction, and thus increase your company’s profit margin.
